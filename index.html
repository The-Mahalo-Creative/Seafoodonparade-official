About Us

A local company operating in the Norwood area for many years, Peter and Koula have a loyal customer base thanks to their friendly service and the freshness of the seafood.

Now trading as Seafood on Parade, their commitment to providing an excellent range of fresh seafood is as strong as ever. Whether it’s fish, oysters, prawns or lobster, you can be assured of the quality.

Large orders, special requests and phone orders are most welcome.

Fresh Produce

Our seafood is market fresh and delivered daily to our store, prepared by our own team for maximum freshness. We always seek fresh over frozen, and proudly stock South Australian wherever possible. Enjoy the finest the state has to offer on your plate.

Largest Range

With a huge range of fresh seafood including fish fillets, raw and cooked prawns, fresh oysters, crabs and more, we guarantee to have something for you. Looking for something a little different? Special requests are most welcome and we will always do our best to accommodate you.

Family Owned

Peter and Koula are brother and sister who took inspiration from their late father George, a fisherman back in homeland Greece, to provide the public with the freshest seafood. Proudly serving the community for over 20 years, providing exceptional service and seafood.
